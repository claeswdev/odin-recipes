Omelette Recipe

Description

A classic dish made from eggs. Add topping of your own choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • A dash of milk
  • 1 tbl Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put the stove to medium heat
  2. Mix the eggs with the milk and stir gently with a fork
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Add butter to the hot pan
  5. Add the egg mix to the pan and cook for a few minutes
  6. When the omelette has become solid, flip the omelette
  7. Extra: Add your topping of choice, for example tomatoes and parsley
