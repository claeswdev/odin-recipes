Chicken Fajita Recipe

Description

This mexican dish is just a delight. Moist chicken and spring paprika works wonders with the spice-mix that is fajita.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 bag of fajita spice-mix
  • 2 paprikas of different colors
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Tortilla
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Chop the onion
  2. Slice the paprika
  3. Slice the chicken
  4. Fry the onion and the paprika with the olive oil in a pan
  5. After the onion and paprika has become soft and gotten some color, add the chicken a cook for a few minutes
  6. Add the fajita-mix and a cup of water and mix together
  7. Eat the fajita with tortilla and your choice of toppings and sauces
