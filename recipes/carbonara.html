Carbonara

Description

To make this classic italian pasta dish, you only need a fex ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Pecorino
  • Guancale
  • Pasta
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Put the guancale in a cold frying pan and heat it
  2. Cook the guancale until it's crispy
  3. Boil the pasta, and save some pasta water
  4. Mix the eggs and the pecorino
  5. When the pasta is done, move it to the guancale and mix
  6. Then add the egg-cheese-mix and stir
  7. Serve right away with a generous amount of black pepper
